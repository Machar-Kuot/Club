About Us

At Kings Sports, we are building the leading basketball brand in Africa. We envision a future where basketball transcends boundaries and African talent shines on the global stage.

Our journey is fueled by an intense passion for the game, and our commitment extends beyond the court. We are actively amplifying the individual, social, and economic impact of youth sports.

We recognize and leverage trends, gaps, and opportunities within the basketball community, and use our convening power to promote African basketball by developing professional teams, promoting youth basketball, and establishing sports facilities.

Tiger Basketball Club

The Firdt South Sudan team to qualify for the Basketball Africa League (BAL).

Two-time unbeaten champions in the South Sudan Basketball Federation Premier League.

All in for the fans. #WeAreTigers

We made history in 2024 - and again in 2025 - by being the first South Sudan team to qualify for the Basketball Africa League (BAL). We also remained undefeated in two straight seasons in the South Sudaness Basketball League (2023-2024 & 2024-2025).

We are more than a winning basketball brand - we are on a mission to transform the landscape of the sport on the continent. We're dedicated to the growth of basketball in South Sudan, East Africa and beyond.
