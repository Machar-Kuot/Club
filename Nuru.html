Our Story

Founded in 2024

Tiger Basketball Club was established in Nairobi, Kenya in 2024 with a vision to develop talented basketball players and promote the sport in East Africa.

From humble beginnings on local courts, we have grown into one of Kenya's most competitive and respected basketball organizations.

Our First Championship

In 2026, just two years after formation, the Tigers won their first Kenyan National Championship, marking the beginning of a legacy of excellence.

Today, we continue to train, compete, and inspire the next generation of Kenyan basketball stars.
